Delete Your Account

Learn how to permanently delete your Braino account

Important Warning

Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, all your data including profiles, learning history, progress tracking, and subscription information will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open the Braino App

Launch the Braino app on your device and ensure you're logged in to the account you want to delete.

2

Navigate to Settings

Tap on the Settings icon located in the bottom navigation bar to access your account settings.

3

Find Delete Account Option

Scroll down in the Settings screen until you find the "Delete Account" option.

4

Confirm Deletion

Tap on "Delete Account" and you'll see a confirmation dialog: "Are you sure? This will permanently delete your account and all data. This action cannot be undone."

5

Final Confirmation

Tap the "Delete" button to permanently delete your account. Your account and all associated data will be immediately removed from our servers.

Additional Information

What Gets Deleted?

  • All user profiles (student accounts)
  • Learning history and progress data
  • Saved questions and chat history
  • Subscription information
  • Account credentials
  • All preferences and settings

Need Help?

  • Contact support before deleting
  • Consider canceling subscription instead
  • You can create a new account later
  • Data won't be restored after deletion
